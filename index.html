Meet Nicole

Mama Public Health

Your Candidate for Student Representation

Hello fellow students! I'm Nicole, a third-year Law student at Kenyatta University, and I'm running to be your next Student Representative.

My journey at KU has shown me both the incredible potential of our student body and the areas where we need stronger advocacy. I've served in various leadership roles, including Class Representative and Legal Aid Club Secretary, where I've fought for student rights and organized successful campus initiatives.

I believe in action over empty promises, inclusion over exclusion, and progress over stagnation. Together, we can build a Kenyatta University that works for all students.
